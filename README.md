### 🪶 About

I'm a full-stack developer and DevOps engineer with **7+ years** designing, building, and operating modern web applications end-to-end — from the database and infrastructure up to the interface people actually touch.

I build with **patience, intention, and respect for the people on the other side of the screen** — a value I carry from my Lakota heritage into every product I ship. When I'm not shipping code, you'll find me at a coffee shop, gaming with my ten-year-old son, or in Lakota Ceremony.

- 🔭 Currently building **[thelakotadev.com](https://thelakotadev.com)** *(beta)* — my platform and the home of **Native StoryBooks**
- 🧠 I like the hard-to-see parts: realtime systems, query optimization, self-managed infra, and a little ML
- 🌱 Grounded in community — I maintain the **[Native Dads Network](https://nativedadsnetwork.org)** site pro bono
- 💬 *Making technology make sense — ask me about anything!*

### 🚀 Currently Building — theLakotaDev.com <sub>(beta)</sub>

My personal platform, built the way I build for clients: a production **Laravel + Inertia + React 19 (SSR)** app with realtime features over **Laravel Reverb**, running on self-managed **AWS EC2** with Apache and automated TLS. It's also the home of **Native StoryBooks** — a living archive where the community can create profiles and **upload, geotag, and manage tribal stories**. *Every story has a place.*
